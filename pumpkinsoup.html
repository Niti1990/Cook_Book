Pumpkin soup

Flavourful and thick, this pumpkin soup will keep you warm and cosy during the cold, cold winter evenings.

Below you can find the table with ingredients for a pumpkin soup.

Type of ingredient Quantity needed
Pumpkin 1000 g
Stock 500 ml
Water 500 ml
Potatoes 500 g
Onions 1 large
Garlic 3 cloves
For serving
Sour cream A splash
Pumpkin seeds 50 g
