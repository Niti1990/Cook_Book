Lasagne

Here is a wonderful lasagne recipe! Just like my granny used to make. For a vegeterian option, swap minced meat for lentils.

Below you can find the table with ingredients for lasagne.

Type of ingredient Quantity needed
Meat 500 g
Pasta 250 g
Canned tomatoes 300 g
Onions 1 big onion
Garlic 3 cloves
For the bechamel sauce
Butter 200 g
Milk 250 ml
Flour 300 g
